I am a research assistant professor at Peking University. Before joining PKU, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University, hosted by Professor Wenwu Zhu. I obtained my PhD under the supervision of Professor Hong Mei and Professor Yasha Wang from Peking University, my master’s degree from the State University of New York, and my bachelor’s degree from the University of Science and Technology of China.

My research field is machine learning and data mining, with a particular interest in algorithms and models for complex real-world data. Currently, I am working on domain generalization and adaptation, healthcare data analytics, graph represenation learning, and large language models, including retrieval-augmented and continual learning methods.
